Judith Shapiro

Former President of Barnard, 1994-2008

Rania Siddique for Spectator

“At Barnard, I would sing at class dinners and at alumnae reunions. And for alumnae reunions, I would sing songs that were popular when they graduated. These would be 50 year old songs—that was a good time for songs. I wouldn’t be very good at singing songs from current students’ times. But I enjoyed that.”
