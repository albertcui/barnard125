Ntozake Shange, Class of 1970

Playwright and poet

Video by Sarah Frost

“I studied American art in the vehicular and it changed my life. It changed my entire perspective of the world and what beauty is … Eventually it became clear that my biggest strength was writing. I couldn't paint so I couldn't do the banners and the fliers, so I started writing poems about the causes I cared about.”
